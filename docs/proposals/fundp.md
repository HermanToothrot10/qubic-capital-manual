## Purpose

To initiate a fundraising round by issuing new QCAP at a fixed price.

## Required inputs

- GitHub link
- Number of QCAP to issue
- Price per QCAP

## Voting & approval
- Quorum required
- Yes votes must exceed No votes

## Execution outcome

If approved, the fundraising round becomes active and remains open until all QCAP are sold.

Multiple fundraising rounds may be active at the same time.
