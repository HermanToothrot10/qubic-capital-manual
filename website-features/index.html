    Website Features

    The platform includes multiple dedicated screens:

    Dashboard

    Provides a set of key metrics and statistics, including:

    • Treasury balances
    • Current smart contract parameters
    • Price action and market capitalization
    • Holders and voting power statistics
    • Holdings breakdown and composition
    • Revenue and distribution metrics

    Proposals

    • Active proposals: can be voted on and tracked in real time
    • Historical proposals: finalized proposals with full results and outcomes

    This ensures full transparency over both governance activity and financial status.

    Wallet

    • Buy QCAP from an active fundraising round (primary market)
    • Transfer asset management rights for QCAP or any other asset to and from QVAULT
    • Lock QCAP to obtain voting power
    • Submit proposals (if eligible)

    These actions are required to fully participate in governance and asset operations.
